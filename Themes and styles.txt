Style:

  --> A style is a collection of attributes that specify the appearence for a View or
      window.
 --> A style can specify the attributes such as font color,font size,background color etc.
   

    <style name="CustomOnstyle">
      <item name="android:layout_width">fill_parent</item>
       <item name="android:layout_height">fill_parent</item>
        <item name="android:textSize">12pt</item>
      </style>

Theme:
    
    A Theme is a collection of attributes that applies on entire app,
     activity,or view hierarchy(not just an individual view)

    Themes can also apply styles to non-view elements,such as status bar
     and window background
